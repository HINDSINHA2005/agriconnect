About the Platform

Connecting farmers directly with the government for fair and transparent crop sales.

For Farmers

Farmers can submit their crop details, access state-wise crop data, and sell directly to the government, bypassing middlemen.

For Government Officials

Government officials can review available crops, make purchases, and ensure a smooth delivery process to state storage units.
